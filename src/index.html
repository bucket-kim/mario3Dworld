Yoshi's Island 1

First Level of the game. It's the only way to reach the Yellow Switch to activate the yellow block. The level has a grassland theme.
Yoshi's Island 2

Second Level of the game. This level introduces more characters, such as Yoshi, Monty Moles, Koopa, Berries, and Beanstalks. The level has a grassland theme.
Yoshi's Island 3

Third Level of the game. It takes place on a high mountain and features yellow blocks, so you can find hidden areas. Also features swing lift and stretch blocks.
Yoshi's Island 4

Fourth Level of the game. It takes place on a lake and features a lot of floating platforms. This level features fire flowers
